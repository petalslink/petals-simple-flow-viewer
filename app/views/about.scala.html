This application allows to visualize Petals exchanges or flows.

Information is extracted and combined from log files generated by Petals. These files require a specific configuration, and more precisely, the MONIT log level must be activated.

Thus, this application also illustrates the possibilities offered by the Petals logs.

You can visit Petals ESB's web site at http://petals.ow2.org
